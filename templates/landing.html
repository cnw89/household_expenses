On this site we're interested in how much income is enough for a UK household.

'Enough for what?' you may ask.

It could be enough for just surviving, or enough for lavish holidays - you decide as we guide you through making a household budget.

For whatever 'enough' you choose, we will make an analysis using Office of National Statistics data to help answer a few questions:

  • What does it take for different household sizes to earn enough?
  • What fraction of UK citizens have enough?
  • How much more or less than enough do the highest and lowest-income households have?
  • Do we have enough for everyone?
