What if there was some time to get on with whatever you wanted to do in the productive hours of your week, without the pressure to earn money then, without being worn out from overwork?
And what if it wasn't just you with the extra time but everyone?

How would our culture be enriched by all those with the inclination to form the band they always wanted to be in, work on art projects or develop craftmanship skills?
How would our relationships be transformed by more time to spend face-to-face with family, friends and lovers?
How much would the resilience of our society be built by having more time to care for each other and work together as communities?

On this site we want to ask How close are we to all having enough to live on and allowing ourselves to work less?

Tell us how much you think is enough and then we will answer:

  • What fraction of UK households have enough?
    • {# list-style-position:inside; #}
  • How much more do those without enough need collectively?
  • How much more than enough do the wealthiest have collectively?
  • How much would the economy have to grow for everyone to have enough without redistributing wealth, and how long might this take?
